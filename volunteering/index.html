Why volunteer with us?

Volunteering with us is a great opportunity for you to expand your skills and provide a priceless service to your local community. By volunteering with us you will be able to build an impressive set of skills for your future, expanding your career opportunities exponentially. Volunteering with us also gives you the opportunity to create long lasting with both your fellow volunteers and your community as a whole and to lift yourself up to become a well-recognised pillar of the community. As an organisation we provide high class training to each and every one of our volunteers to prepare them for both their time as a volunteer and for their futures. Requirements for volunteering

In order to volunteer with us you must be/have:

  • At least 18 years old
  • Good English and Maths skills
  • A good understanding of sports, fitness and nutrition
  • Excellent interpersonal skills
  • Motivated, dedicated and happy to help out whenever you can

Training will be provided for some areas if needed. If you feel you meet these requirements please fill out the form below.
